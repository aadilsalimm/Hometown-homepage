Kerala

Kerala is a small state in the southwestern coast of India, constituting only about 1% of the total area of the country. It stretches for about 360 miles along the Malabar Coast, varying in width from roughly 20 to 75 miles.

Things to do in Kerala

Boat Riding

Boat riding is one of the most interesting thing to do in Kerala. You could spend a day riding the boat. Make sure not to do this on noon.

Elephant Riding

Elephant is one of the best things to ride. Do not enter the forest and try to ride a random elephant by your own (optional).

Watching kathakali

Want some entertainment? Go watch Kathakali. Kathakali literally means "story-play". which means the play is about some stories (obviously).

Scuba-diving

Even I did'nt knew that scuba-diving existed in Kerala! Anyways, go check it out and if you make out alive, don't forget to rate us 5 stars.
